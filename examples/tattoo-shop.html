About the Artist

Meet Your Artist

Marcus "Cipher" Chen

Transforming skin into canvas since 2016. Specializing in blackwork, geometric patterns, and fine line realism.

What started as a passion for drawing evolved into a lifelong dedication to the art of tattooing. After apprenticing under legendary artists in Tokyo and Berlin, I founded Cipher Ink Studio with one mission: to create meaningful art that stands the test of time.

Every tattoo I create is a collaboration between artist and client. Your vision, your story, your skin - my hands, my expertise, our masterpiece.

Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

Fill out the booking form on this page or DM us on Instagram. Include your idea, preferred size/placement, and reference images if you have them. We'll get back to you within 48 hours to schedule a consultation.

Our minimum is $150. Small pieces typically range $150-300, medium pieces $350-600, and larger work is $180/hour. Final pricing depends on complexity, detail, and placement. We provide exact quotes after consultation.

We accept walk-ins for flash designs when we have availability. For custom work, appointments are required. Follow us on Instagram for flash day announcements!

Get a good night's sleep, eat a full meal before your appointment, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours. Wear comfortable clothing that provides easy access to the tattoo area. Bring snacks for longer sessions!

Initial healing takes 2-3 weeks. You'll experience some redness, swelling, and peeling - this is normal. Full healing takes 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and premium aftercare products with every tattoo.
