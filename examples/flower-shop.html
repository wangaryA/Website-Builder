Our Story

Two Friends, One Slow December Afternoon

It all started with paper petals folded to pass time in art class.

We're Maya and Lila, two high schoolers who bonded over a shared love of creating. One December afternoon, we started folding colorful paper into petals just for fun. A teacher noticed and asked if we could make a bouquet for a retirement party.

That bouquet sparked everything.

We stayed up after homework perfecting folds, studying origami techniques, experimenting with glue types, and learning how lighting affects the look of our petals. Friends started requesting custom pieces. Then the school leadership club needed decorations for an event‚Äîwe created over fifty oversized blossoms using recycled materials.

The display went viral on the school's social media. From that moment, the idea of a real brand clicked.
