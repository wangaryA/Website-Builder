Our Story

A Small Studio with Big Attention to Detail

Velvet Touch Nails started as a simple idea: quality nail care should feel like self care. Our founder, Mia Park, trained in cosmetic nail science and professional nail art. She opened the studio to create a calm, welcoming space where clients can get beautiful nails while learning how to keep them healthy.

We focus on gentle prep, proper shaping, and techniques that protect the natural nail. We use safer formulations and industry tested sterilization. Our team updates training every quarter so we can bring the latest safe trends to our clients.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should I expect my appointment to take?

Classic manicures are about 35 to 45 minutes. Gel manicures run 50 to 70 minutes. Full sets or detailed art may take 90 to 120 minutes.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We accept limited walk-ins based on availability. Booking online guarantees your preferred time.

Are your products vegan or cruelty-free?

We use a selection of vegan and cruelty-free polishes and acetone-free removers when possible. If you have product concerns please ask at booking.

Can I bring reference photos for my nail art?

Yes! Photos help us match designs. Please bring them on your phone and keep in mind some designs take extra time and cost more.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a 50% fee. No-show fee is 50% of service price.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! For group or event bookings, a 30% non-refundable deposit is required. Contact us for bridal parties and special events.
