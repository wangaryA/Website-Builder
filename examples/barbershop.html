About Us

The Shop Built for Confidence and Community

Prime Edge Barbers started with a simple goal. Build a shop where clients feel understood and appreciated. Our founders, Marcus and Devin, grew up cutting friends' hair in their garage before professionally training and opening a barbershop that blends craftsmanship with a welcoming atmosphere.

We take the craft seriously. Every barber on our team trains in classic cuts, modern fades, beard sculpting, and razor work. We believe consistency matters. When you sit in our chair, you get a barber who studies your hair type, listens to what you want, and executes with precision.
